About Me

Meet Josh Payne

Josh is the Owner and Instructor here at Prime Time Performance. He is certified by the National Academy of Sports Medicine as a Personal Trainer, Performance Enhancement Specialist, and Youth Exercise Specialist. He also hold a Master's of Science in Social Work. His goal in creating Prime Time was to provide the community with a place for families to build a healthy relationship with fitness. This mission derives for his personal journey with fitness, a love of sports, and experience serving youth in both the academic and non-profit industries. While working with Josh, you can expect his magnetic energy to propel you further than you ever imagined possible.
